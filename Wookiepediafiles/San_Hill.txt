'''San Hill''' was a [[Sexes|male]] [[Muun]] and [[Chairman]] of the [[InterGalactic Banking Clan]] during the [[Clone Wars]]. He pledged the support of the Banking Clan to the [[Confederacy of Independent Systems]] and served as the financial cartel's representative on the [[Separatist Council]]. Hill was slaughtered by [[Anakin Skywalker|Darth Vader]] on [[Mustafar]] following the end of the Clone Wars.

==Biography==
A [[Sexes|male]] [[Muun]] from the [[planet]] [[Scipio]], San Hill was the [[Chairman]] of the [[InterGalactic Banking Clan]].<ref name="Ultimate Star Wars" /> On the onset of the [[Clone Wars]],<ref name="San Hill" /> Hill attended a secret meeting on [[Geonosis]]<ref name="Attack of the Clones" /> and signed a treaty with the [[Confederacy of Independent Systems]],<ref name="San Hill" /> pledging the Banking Clan's financial and military assets to the movement,<ref name="Card Trader">[[Star Wars: Card Trader|''Star Wars'': Card Trader]]</ref> with Hill serving as the financial cartel's representative on the [[Separatist Council]].<ref name="San Hill" /> During the final [[Standard day|days]] of the Clone Wars, Hill, along with the other [[Separatist Council|Separatist leaders]], moved from [[Utapau]] to [[Mustafar]]<ref name="Revenge of the Sith">[[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']]</ref> where they were all [[Death|killed]] by [[Anakin Skywalker|Darth Vader]].<ref name="Ultimate Star Wars" />

==Personality and traits==
Like most Muuns, San Hill was more comfortable with business and closed-door [[politics]] than being on the front lines of war. Hill was highly influential, and wasn't above conspiring with others to get his way.<ref name="Card Trader" />

A male Muun, Hill stood at 1.91 [[meter]]s tall,<ref name="San Hill" /> and had [[Color|yellow]] [[eye]]s and pale skin.<ref name="Attack of the Clones" />

==Behind the scenes==
San Hill first appeared in the [[2002]] film [[Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones|''Star Wars'': Episode II ''Attack of the Clones'']], the second installment of the ''[[Star Wars]]'' [[prequel trilogy]].<ref name="Attack of the Clones" /> Hill then appeared once more in [[2005]]'s [[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']].<ref name="Revenge of the Sith" /> The character's gaunt form is draped in CG wardrobe developed by concept artist [[Dermot Power]].<ref name="databank">{{DB|character|sanhill|San Hill}}</ref>
