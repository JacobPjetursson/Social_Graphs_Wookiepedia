"'''Dogma'''" was the nickname of a [[clone trooper]] who served in the [[501st Legion]] of the [[Grand Army of the Republic]] during the [[Clone Wars]].<ref name="Darkness on Umbara" /> He was known to always follow orders. After being manipulated by [[Confederacy of Independent Systems|Separatist]] agent [[Pong Krell]] during the [[Battle of Umbara]], Dogma [[Death|executed]] Krell to prevent him from revealing [[Galactic Republic|Republic]] military secrets.<ref name="Carnage of Krell">{{TCW|Carnage of Krell}}</ref> Dogma wore [[phase II clone trooper armor]] with blue markings and used a [[DC-15A blaster|DC-15A]] [[blaster]].
