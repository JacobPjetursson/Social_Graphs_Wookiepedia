'''CC-1993''', nicknamed "'''Jet'''," was a [[clone trooper]] [[commander]] and a heavy weapons specialist who served in the [[Grand Army of the Republic]] during the [[Clone Wars]]. He notably led a unit of [[clone flametrooper]]s and other special operations [[trooper]]s in the [[Second Battle of Geonosis]].<ref name="Encyclopedia" /> He served under [[Jedi General]] [[Ki-Adi-Mundi]] and fought in the [[Second Battle of Geonosis|battle]] to retake the [[planet]] [[Geonosis]] from the [[Confederacy of Independent Systems]].<ref name="Landing at Point Rain" />
