'''Apailana''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] [[Monarchy|monarch]] who served as the [[Monarch of Naboo|Queen]] of [[Naboo]] during the final [[Standard day|days]] of the [[Clone Wars]] and the early days of the [[Galactic Empire]].<ref name="Episode III" /> She was one of the youngest rulers to be [[Politics|elected]] by [[Naboo (people)|the Naboo]], succeeding [[Neeyutnee]] at the age of twelve.<ref name="ECUC" />

Shortly after the Clone Wars, the young [[queen]] attended the [[Funeral of Padmé Amidala|funeral]] of [[Senator]] [[Padmé Amidala]],<ref name="Episode III" /> who had supported Apailana's election as the new monarch. However, she did not believe the official story of Amidala's [[Death|demise]], which claimed that rogue [[Jedi]] had [[assassin]]ated the senator.<ref name="ECUC" />

==Personality and traits==
Apailana measured 1.57 [[meter]]s and had [[Color|brown]] [[eye]]s and light skin.<ref name="ECUC" /> During Amidala's funeral, she wore a headdress and put on makeup and like other queens, a [[scar of remembrance]].<ref name="Episode III" />

==Behind the scenes==
Apailana was portrayed by the [[wikipedia:Academy Award|Oscar]]-nominated [[Wikipedia:New Zealander|New Zealander]] actress [[Keisha Castle-Hughes]].<ref name="Episode III" /> Castle-Hughes spent seven hours in make-up for her role in Episode III and filmed her scene in only one day.<ref name="IMDBBio">{{Imdb|url=name/nm1095720/bio|text=Keisha Castle-Hughes &ndash; Biography}}</ref>
