'''Momaw Nadon''', also known as "'''Hammerhead'''," was a [[Sexes|male]] [[Ithorian]] who was [[Exile (punishment)|exiled]] from his [[homeworld]] of [[Ithor]] as punishment for revealing the agricultural secrets of his [[species]] to the [[Galactic Empire]] in order to save the [[planet]]. He spent his exile on [[Tatooine]] and sympathized with the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic]] during the [[Galactic Civil War]].

==Biography==
Momaw Nadon [[Homeworld|hailed]] from the [[planet]] [[Ithor]], until [[Sexes|he]] was [[Exile (punishment)|exiled]] as punishment for revealing [[Ithorian|his]] [[species]]' agricultural secrets to the [[Galactic Empire]] in order to save Ithor. Spending his exile on the [[desert]] world of [[Tatooine]], Nadon maintained a small [[Momaw Nadon's garden|secret garden]] in the [[mountain]]s south of the planet's [[Mos Eisley]] [[spaceport]]. During the [[Galactic Civil War]] he sympathised with the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic]] who opposed the Empire, and would hide Rebel operatives in his garden.<ref name="Ultimate Star Wars" /> Nadon was present in [[Chalmun's Cantina]] when [[Jedi Master]] [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]] and local farm boy [[Luke Skywalker]] arrived in search of a [[pilot]] to take them to the planet [[Alderaan]]. He witnessed the pair enter into an altercation with [[Doctor|Dr.]] [[Cornelius Evazan]] and his companion [[Ponda Baba]], which ended with Kenobi [[Dismemberment|dismembering]] Baba and wounding Evazan with [[Obi-Wan Kenobi's lightsaber (Jedi Master)|his]] [[lightsaber]].<ref name="A New Hope" />

==Behind the scenes==
Momaw Nadon first appeared in the [[1977]] film [[Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope|''Star Wars'': Episode IV ''A New Hope'']] but went unnamed<ref name="A New Hope" /> until identified as "Hammerhead" for his [[Kenner Products]] action figure.<ref name="Kenner">''[[Star Wars (toy line)|Star Wars]]''</ref> He received his name and backstory in ''[[Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope]]'', a [[1989]] [[Star Wars Legends|''Star Wars'' Legends]] sourcebook written by [[Michael Stern]] and [[Grant S. Boucher]] for ''[[Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game]]''.<ref name="Galaxy Guide 1">''[[Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope]]''</ref> His name and portions of his backstory were confirmed to be [[canon]] by the reference book ''[[Ultimate Star Wars]]'', which was released in [[2015]].<ref name="Ultimate Star Wars" />
