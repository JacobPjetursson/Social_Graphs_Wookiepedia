'''Mama''' was an elder obese [[Sexes|female]] [[Hutt]] of the [[Hutt Clan]] prior to and during the [[Clone Wars]]. She was the mother of [[Ebor]] and [[Ziro Desilijic Tiure/Canon|Ziro]], the grandmother of [[Jabba Desilijic Tiure|Jabba]], and the great-grandmother of [[Rotta]] and [[Gorga Desilijic Aarrpo|Gorga]].
