'''Caluan Ematt''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|male]] [[soldier]] who served as a [[lieutenant]] in the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic]] during the [[Galactic Civil War]]. He fought against the [[Galactic Empire]], during which Ematt was given [[Commander|command]] of the [[Shrikes]], a special recon unit that was tasked with identifying, securing and preparing new Rebel bases for the Alliance. He was a friend of [[Prince]]ss [[Leia Organa]] and would later go on to serve with her at a [[Resistance base]] as a [[major]], and later [[general]] in the [[Resistance]] during the [[First Order–Resistance war|war]] against the [[First Order]]

==Biography==
===Galactic Civil War===
[[File:Lt Ematt.jpg|thumb|left|Ematt as a young Lieutenant.]]
[[Lieutenant]] Caluan Ematt was a [[Rebel trooper|soldier]] in the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic]] during its fight against the [[Galactic Empire]] in the [[Galactic Civil War]] serving as part of the [[Shrikes]] recon unit.<ref name="Smuggler's Run" /> Shortly after the [[Battle of Hoth]], Ematt accompanied [[Nien Nunb]] to the ''[[Remembrance]]'' so that they could pick [[Leia Organa]] up and take [[Sexes|her]] to a secret meeting with the [[Alliance High Command|leadership]] of the Alliance to Restore the Republic on [[Zastiga]].<ref name="Moving Target" /> Ematt was later part of the [[Endor strike team]] led by [[General]] [[Han Solo]], and participated in the climactic [[Battle of Endor]].<ref name="Rebel Files" />

===Joining the Resistance===
[[File:Major Ematt edit.png|thumb|right|200px|Ematt as a Resistance Major]]
Eventually the Alliance to Restore the Republic was able to defeat the Empire and was able to form the [[New Republic]] and Ematt became a member of it.<ref name="Bloodline" /> In [[28 ABY]],<ref name="Timeframe">{{Twitter|DelReyStarWars|status/652893780236988420|[[Del Rey]]|quote=So excited to have @claudiagray writing a #StarWars novel with us. SW: New Republic: Bloodline coming 2016. Set 6 years before TFA.|image=File:New Republic Bloodline Twitter Post.jpg}} The tweet in question states that the events of ''Bloodline'' take place six [[Standard year|years]] before the events of the film [[Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens|''Star Wars'': Episode VII ''The Force Awakens'']]. ''[[Star Wars: Galactic Atlas]]'' establishes that the events of ''The Force Awakens'' take place in the year [[34 ABY]] and so using simple math it can be concluded that the events of ''Bloodline'' take place in [[28 ABY]].</ref> following the [[Tai-Lin Garr's assassination|assassination]] of [[Senator]] [[Tai-Lin Garr]], Ematt attended a secret meeting with several other [[New Republic soldier]]s, [[pilot]]s, and [[officer]]s where Leia announced the formation of the [[Resistance]], a paramilitary organization dedicated to defending the [[New Republic]] from threats like the [[First Order]].<ref name="Bloodline" />

Over the [[Standard year|years]],<ref name="TFA Timeline">''[[Star Wars: The Force Awakens: The Visual Dictionary]]'' notes that [[Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens|''Star Wars'': Episode VII ''The Force Awakens'']] occurs 30 years after the [[Battle of Endor]], which takes place during [[Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi|''Star Wars'': Episode VI ''Return of the Jedi'']].</ref> Ematt would see many [[New Republic officer|Republic officers]] leaving the service to join the Resistance. He would serve the Resistance's leader Organa, who was at that point a general.<ref name="ECUC" />

Sometime after, he escorted General Organa to the [[Mirrin Prime Republic Base]] to see the recruitment of [[Commander]] [[Poe Dameron]], fresh from a [[Skirmish in OR-Kappa-2722|skirmish]] with the First Order. Ematt intercepted the young pilot as he landed with two [[shore police]], and brought him to the General.<ref name="Before">''[[Before the Awakening]]''</ref>

====Battle of Crait====
{{Quote|Ground forces, incoming.|Caluan Ematt during the Battle of Crait|Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi}}
[[File:Ematt @ Crait.jpg|thumb|left|200px|General Ematt with his troops on Crait.]]
Following the [[Battle of Starkiller Base]], Ematt was promoted from [[major]] to general.<ref name="Visual Dictionary">''[[Star Wars: The Last Jedi: The Visual Dictionary]]''</ref> During the [[Battle of Crait]], Ematt observed the [[All Terrain MegaCaliber Six]]es en route to [[Crait outpost]], and was situated near a trench while his men took positions.<ref name="Episode VIII">[[Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi|''Star Wars'': Episode VIII ''The Last Jedi'']]</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Caluan Ematt first appeared in the simultaneous release of ''[[Smuggler's Run: A Han Solo & Chewbacca Adventure]]'' and ''[[Moving Target: A Princess Leia Adventure]]'' on [[Force Friday]] in [[2015]], identified only as "Ematt." Later that [[September]], [[Wikipedia:Nestlé|Nestlé]] released ''[[Smuggler's Run (animated comic)|Smuggler's Run]]'', an animated adaptation of the novel, in which Ematt's first name is identified as "Caluan." [[Jordan D. White]], an editor for the ''[[Star Wars]]'' comics at [[Marvel Comics|Marvel]] who worked on the video, confirmed the spelling on [https://twitter.com/cracksh0t/status/649295573229654016 Twitter]. He was portrayed by [[Andrew Jack]] in [[Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens|''Star Wars'': Episode VII ''The Force Awakens'']] and [[Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi|''Star Wars'': Episode VIII ''The Last Jedi'']].
