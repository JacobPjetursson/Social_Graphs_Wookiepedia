'''Pooja Naberrie''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] [[youngling]] around the [[time]] of the [[Galactic Republic]]'s [[Proclamation of the New Order|transition]] into the [[Galactic Empire]] at the [[Empire Day|end]] of the [[Clone Wars]]. The daughter of [[Sola Naberrie]] and sister to [[Ryoo Naberrie|Ryoo]],{{Fact}} she attended the [[Funeral of Padmé Amidala|funeral]] of her maternal aunt, [[Padmé Amidala]] on [[Naboo]] along with other members of her [[Naberrie family|family]]. Naberrie had [[Color|brown]] [[hair]], brown [[eye]]s, and light skin.<ref name="Episode III" />

==Behind the scenes==
Pooja Naberrie was played by the actress [[Hayley Mooy]]. She first appeared in a scene from [[Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones|''Star Wars'': Episode II ''Attack of the Clones'']], but this scene was deleted and not considered [[canon]]. She appears at the end of [[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']] attending Padmé Amidala's funeral. The character's name is given in the film's closing credits.
