'''Ebe E. Endocott''' was a [[Triffian]] [[podracer]] from [[Triffis]] who participated in the [[Boonta Eve Classic]] race on the [[planet]] [[Tatooine]] during the [[Invasion of Naboo|Naboo Crisis]]. Endocott successfully completed the race in fourth place, being beaten by the [[human]] [[Anakin Skywalker]], the [[Xexto]] [[Gasgano]] and the [[Glymphid]] [[Aldar Beedo]].<ref name="AEYNTK" />
