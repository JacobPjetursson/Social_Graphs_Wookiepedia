'''Chi Eekway Papanoida''' was a [[Sexes|female]] [[Pantoran]] and the daughter of [[Baron]] [[Papanoida]] who served as [[Chairman]] of [[Pantora]]. She served as [[senator]] of [[Pantora]] in the [[Galactic Senate]] at the end of the [[Clone Wars]].

==Biography==
During the [[Clone Wars]], [[Pantora]] was placed under a [[Blockade of Pantora|blockade]]. This led to [[Sexes|her]] being [[Kidnapping of the Papanoida daughters|abducted]] by [[Greedo]] and a [[Unidentified Gotal bounty hunter|Gotal]] on orders from the [[Trade Federation]] along with her sister [[Che Amanwe Papanoida]]. Eekway was im[[prison]]ed on the Federation's [[flagship]]. There she lingered for [[Standard day|days]] alone and afraid until [[Senator]] [[Riyo Chuchi]] and [[Jedi]] [[Padawan]] [[Ahsoka Tano]] came to her [[Rescue on the Pantora Droid Control Ship|rescue]]. Papanoida then returned to the [[Galactic Republic]]'s [[capital]], she later attended the senate meetings where the blockade was lifted from her [[planet]].<ref name="SoI">{{TCW|Sphere of Influence}}</ref>

By the [[Standard year|year]] [[19 BBY]],<ref name="Galactic Atlas">''[[Star Wars: Galactic Atlas]]''</ref> Papanoida had succeeded Chuchi as senator of Pantora.<ref name="Trivia Gallery">{{SW|url=tv-shows/clone-wars/sphere-of-influence-trivia-gallery|text=Sphere of Influence Trivia Gallery}} {{C|Slide 2}}</ref> Following his [[Battle of Coruscant|abduction]], [[Supreme Chancellor]] [[Darth Sidious|Palpatine]] came back to a royal welcome as he was greeted by several senators; Chi Eekway was among these distinguished [[ambassador]]s. She stood by [[Terr Taneel]] and wore [[Color|red]] [[robe]]s covering her back and head.<ref name="RotS">[[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']]</ref>

[[File:Papanoida at the opera.jpg|thumb|left|150px|Eekway with her father at the Opera.]]
Eekway attended the [[Galaxies Opera House]] with her [[Papanoida|father]] and watched the [[Squid Lake|performance]]. For the event Chi dressed in a brown velvet cloak and a black floor length dress, and she also wore [[moon]] [[crystal]]s.<ref name="RotS" />

Near the close of the Clone Wars, Eekway joined the [[Delegation of 2,000]],<ref name="PABG" /> a group of senators who demanded that after the war came to an end, Chancellor Palpatine return his emergency powers to the senate.<ref name="DBMT">{{Databank|meena-tills|Meena Tills}}</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Chi Eekway Papanoida was portrayed by one of [[George Lucas]]'s daughters, [[Katie Lucas]]. She was voiced in the ''Clone Wars'' by [[Nika Futterman]].
