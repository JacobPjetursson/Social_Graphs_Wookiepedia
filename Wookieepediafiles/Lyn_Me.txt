'''Lyn Me''' was a [[Twi'lek]] [[Sexes|female]] dancer who performed with the [[Max Rebo Band]] at [[Jabba Desilijic Tiure|Jabba]] the [[Hutt]]'s [[Jabba's Palace|Palace]],<ref name="DB Lyn" /> and sang vocals along with [[Greeata Jendowanian]], [[Sy Snootles]] and [[Rystáll Sant]].<ref name="ROTJ">[[Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi|''Star Wars'': Episode VI ''Return of the Jedi'']]</ref>

Me grew up on the barren [[Ryloth's northern continent|northern continent]] of the [[planet]] [[Ryloth]]. She was saved from [[slavery]] by [[Boba Fett]] and thus hero-worshiped him. She was well-learned in the art of Twi'lek dance.<ref name="Character Encyclopedia" />

==Behind the scenes==
Lyn Me was portrayed by [[Dalyn Chew]]. She was added to the extended musical number in the [[The Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition|Special Edition]] of ''[[Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi|Return of the Jedi]]''.<ref name="Old DB">{{DB|character|lynme|Lyn Me}}</ref>
