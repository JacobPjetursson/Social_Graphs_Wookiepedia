'''TC-14''' was a [[TC-series protocol droid|TC-series]] [[protocol droid]] displaying [[Sexes|feminine programming]] that served aboard the stationed aboard the [[Trade Federation]] [[flagship]], the [[Lucrehulk-class LH-3210 cargo freighter]] ''[[Saak'ak]]''. Due to the nature of her job, her memory banks were wiped regularly to prevent her from developing a personality or conscience.<ref name="AEYNtK" /> She greeted the [[Jedi]] [[Qui-Gon Jinn]] and [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]] when they boarded the vessel and then informed her masters that she believed their guests to be Jedi.<ref name="TPM" />
