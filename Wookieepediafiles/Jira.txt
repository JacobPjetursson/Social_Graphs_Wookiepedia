'''Jira''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] from [[Tatooine]]. At the time of the [[Invasion of Naboo|Naboo Crisis]], the elderly Jira sold [[fruit]]s, such as [[pallie]]s, in the [[Mos Espa]] [[Mos Espa Market|marketplace]]. She was a friend of [[Anakin Skywalker]], then a nine-year-old [[Slavery|slave]] boy.

==Biography==
In the last decades of the [[Galactic Republic]], Jira lived on the [[desert]] [[planet]] of [[Tatooine]], in the [[Outer Rim Territories]]. At the time of the [[Invasion of Naboo|Naboo Crisis]], she ran a small stand in the [[Mos Espa Market|market]] place of [[Mos Espa]], where she sold various [[Foodstuff|food]] products, including [[pallie]]s. Jira was a friend to [[Anakin Skywalker]], then a nine-year-old [[Slavery|slave]] boy. When the elderly woman expressed her need for a [[cooling unit]], Skywalker offered to search for one on her behalf.<ref name="DB" /> When her young friend Skywalker met the [[Jedi Master]] [[Qui-Gon Jinn]], Jira sold them a few pallies. Feeling in her old bones that a [[sandstorm]] was about to occur, she advised Skywalker to get home as soon as possible. A few [[Standard day|days]] later, Jira's young friend left Tatooine with Qui-Gon Jinn.<ref name="Episode I" />

==Personality and traits==
Jira was a [[Color|green]]-[[eye]]d<ref name="Episode I" /> [[human]] [[Sexes|female]].<ref name="SWE" /> In her old age, she tied up her gray [[hair]] into loose braids.<ref name="Episode I" /> Despite her poverty, Jira was still a jovial woman. She was very fond of her young friend Anakin Skywalker, who she called "the kindest boy in [[the galaxy]]."<ref name="DB" />
