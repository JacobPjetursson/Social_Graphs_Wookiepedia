'''Bargwill Tomder''' was a [[Sexes|male]] [[Cloddogran]]<ref name="Hear Nothing, See Nothing, Say Nothing" /> with [[Color|brown]] skin and [[hair]]. Tomder wore a red [[Clothing|shirt]] with a vest over it and a brown [[hat]].<ref name="Card Trader" />

==Personality and traits==
A [[Cloddogran]] groom, Bargwill Tomder ruled the [[fathier]] stables of at the [[Canto Bight racetrack]] with an [[iron]] fist.<ref name="Temiri Blagg">{{Databank|temiri-blagg|Temiri Blagg}}</ref> He was ill-tempered, and his default approach to solving problems was simply to crack his [[whip]].<ref name="TLJ VD">''[[Star Wars: The Last Jedi: The Visual Dictionary]]''</ref>

==Behind the scenes==
Bargwill Tomder was created for the [[2017]] film [[Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi|''Star Wars'': Episode VIII ''The Last Jedi'']]. The character was first revealed through the ''[[Star Wars: Card Trader]]'' app.<ref name="Card Trader" />
