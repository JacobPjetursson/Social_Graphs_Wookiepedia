'''Kitster Banai''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|male]] [[Slavery|slave]] who lived on the [[Outer Rim Territories|Outer Rim]] [[Planet|world]] of [[Tatooine]] during the [[Invasion of Naboo|Naboo Crisis]]. As a child, he befriended [[Anakin Skywalker]]&mdash;a fellow slave and [[podracing]] enthusiast. Banai supported Skywalker as a member of his pit team at the [[Boonta Eve Classic]]. As a result of winning the event, Banai's friend gained his freedom and subsequently departed the [[desert]] planet to join the [[Jedi Order]].

==Biography==
Kitster Banai was a child<ref name="Episode I" /> [[Slavery|enslaved]]<ref name="SWE" /> on [[Tatooine]], an [[Outer Rim Territories|Outer Rim]] [[planet]] that was too distant to be affected by the anti-slave laws of the [[Galactic Republic]].<ref name="Episode I" /> [[Reproduction|Born]] during the final [[Standard year|years]] of the Republic, he was around the same age as his friend, [[Anakin Skywalker]].<ref name="Databank" /> Like Banai,<ref name="SWE" /> Skywalker was also a slave&mdash;having been sold to the [[Toydarian]] [[junk dealer]] [[Watto]].<ref name="Episode I" /> As children, Banai and Skywalker protected each other while braving the dangerous streets of [[Mos Espa]].<ref name="SWE" /> When Skywalker entered his [[Anakin Skywalker's Podracer|custom-built podracer]] in the [[Boonta Eve Classic]] [[podracing]] event,<ref name="Episode I" /> Banai supported him as a member of his pit team.<ref name="Databank" /> Skywalker ultimately won the race along with his freedom, leading Banai and his childhood friend to part ways as Skywalker embarked on a journey to become a [[Jedi]].<ref name="Episode I" />

==Personality and traits==
As a child, Kitster Banai had a positive outlook<ref name="Databank" /> which he shared with his friend Anakin Skywalker.<ref name="Episode I" /> He was often supportive of Skywalker,<ref name="SWE" /> both prior to<ref name="Episode I" /> and during the Boonta Eve Classic.<ref name="Databank" /> Although victory had alluded Skywalker in previous racing events, he offered encouraging words nonetheless.<ref name="Episode I" /> Despite his optimism,<ref name="Databank" /> he was also doubtful as to whether or not Skywalker could repair his podracer, let alone win the race.<ref name="SWE" /> Even so, Banai attended the event<ref name="Episode I" /> where he cheered on his friend.<ref name="SWE" />

Kitster Bani was a [[human]] [[Sexes|male]]<ref name="Databank" /> who possessed [[Color|black]] [[hair]], brown [[eye]]s, and tan skin.<ref name="Episode I" /> During his childhood, he had a height of 1.24 [[meter]]s.<ref name="Databank" />
