==Behind the scenes==
Pharl McQuarrie's name is a [[tuckerization]] of the late [[Ralph McQuarrie]], who was the conceptual designer responsible for much of the aesthetics of and many designs featured in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' [[original trilogy]].
