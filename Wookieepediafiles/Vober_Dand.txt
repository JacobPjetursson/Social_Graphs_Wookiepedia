'''Vober Dand''' was a [[Tarsunt]] [[Sexes|male]]<ref name="TFAVD" /> from [[Suntilla]]<ref name="Character Encyclopedia" /> who served in the [[Resistance]]'s [[Ground Logistics Division]] as a [[controller]]. Around thirty [[Standard year|years]] after the [[Battle of Endor]],<ref name="TFAVD" /> he was stationed in the Resistance's [[Resistance base|base of operations]] on the [[planet]] [[D'Qar]] and was present during the preparations and execution of the Resistance's [[Battle of Starkiller Base|attack]] on the [[First Order]]'s [[Starkiller Base]].<ref name="TFA">[[Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens|''Star Wars'': Episode VII ''The Force Awakens'']]</ref>

During the [[evacuation of D'Qar]], Dand was stationed on the Resistance [[flagship]], [[Raddus (MC85 Star Cruiser)|''Raddus'']]. He was present when [[Vice Admiral]] [[Amilyn Holdo]] was given command of the Resistance, and listened to her introductory speech.<ref name="Episode VIII">[[Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi|''Star Wars'': Episode VIII ''The Last Jedi'']]</ref>

Minutes before the speech he had been arguing with Resistance [[Poe Dameron|Captain Poe Dameron]] about the best way they could reshuffle the fleet's remaining starfighters to defend the Raddus.<ref name="Expanded Edition">''[[Star Wars: The Last Jedi: Expanded Edition]]''</ref>

==Personality and traits==
Dand was a stickler for detail and refused to tolerate anyone in the base operating outside of protocol. He wore a [[Color|yellow]] GLD controller's coat with a pair of brown [[pants]] and [[boot]]s as well as a [[comlink]] headset. Dand had brown [[hair]], black [[eye]]s and gray skin.<ref name="TFAVD" />

==Behind the scenes==
Vober Dand first appeared in the film [[Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens|''Star Wars'': Episode VII ''The Force Awakens'']] which was released in [[2015]]. He was not identified in the film, but received a name in ''[[Star Wars: The Force Awakens: The Visual Dictionary]]'', which was written by [[Pablo Hidalgo]] and released the same day as the film. He was portrayed by [[Derek Arnold]] in the film, but was not listed in the end credits.
