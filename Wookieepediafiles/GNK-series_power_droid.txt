The '''GNK-series power droid''', also known as the '''gonk droid''', was a type of well-known [[power droid]]<ref name="Databank" /> manufactured by [[Industrial Automaton]] that used binary codes.<ref name="Character Encyclopedia" /> They were often referred to as Gonk droids in imitation of their simple vocalizations.<ref name="Databank" /> The [[Alliance to Restore the Republic]] modified GNK units so they acted as weapons. Once deployed those droids would march slowly across the battlefield, until they collided with an enemy building, and explode.<ref name="FA">''[[Star Wars: Force Arena]]''</ref>
