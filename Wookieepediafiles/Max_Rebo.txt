'''Max Rebo''' was a [[Sexes|male]] [[Ortolan]] from [[Orto]]<ref name="Ultimate Star Wars" /> and a popular musician who led the [[Max Rebo Band]]. He mainly played the [[red ball jett organ]],<ref name="Max Rebo" /> which he played with his [[Limb|feet]];<ref name="AEYKTK">''[[Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know]]''</ref> he and his band often performed for criminals and otherwise sleazy clientele.<ref name="Max Rebo" />
