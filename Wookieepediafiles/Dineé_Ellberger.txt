'''Dineé Ellberger'''<ref name="Visual Encyclopedia">''[[Star Wars: The Visual Encyclopedia]]''</ref> was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] [[Naboo pilot|pilot]] who flew an [[N-1 starfighter]] as part of [[Bravo Flight]] during the [[Invasion of Naboo|Invasion]] of [[Naboo]].<ref name="Episode I" />

As a [[pilot]] of the [[Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps]], Ellberger wore a pilot uniform consisting of a space fighter corps [[Coat|overcoat]], a [[Flight jacket|flying jacket]] and [[glove]]s, and pilot-issue [[boot]]s.<ref name="Visual Encyclopedia" />
