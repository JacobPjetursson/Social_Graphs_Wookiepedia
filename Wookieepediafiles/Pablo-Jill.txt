'''Pablo-Jill'''<ref name="SWCT" /> was a [[Sexes|male]]<ref name="Force Collection" /> [[Ongree]]<ref name="Episode II" /> [[Jedi Knight]]<ref name="Force Collection" />  that participated in the [[Battle of Geonosis]] as a part of the [[Jedi assault team]]. Pablo-Jill fought alongside the Jedi in an attempt to free [[Jedi Knight]]s [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]], and [[Anakin Skywalker]], along with [[Senator]] [[Padmé Amidala]] from the [[Confederacy of Independent Systems]].<ref name="Episode II" /> [[Time|Sometime]] during the [[Clone Wars]], [[Pablo-Jill's lightsaber]] was stolen from floating debris by Separatist [[General]] [[Grievous]] following an intense [[Duel over Duro|duel]] over [[Duro]] in a [[Unidentified satellite city|satellite city]] that left Pablo-Jill injured.<ref name="Lightsabers">''[[Star Wars Lightsabers: A Guide to Weapons of the Force]]''</ref> Though encouraged by the [[Jedi Order]] to take a [[padawan]], Pablo-Jill never chose one.<ref name="Force Collection" />

==Equipment==
Pablo-Jill wore [[Jedi robes]] and owned a [[Color|blue]] [[Pablo-Jill's lightsaber|lightsaber]].<ref name="Episode II" />
