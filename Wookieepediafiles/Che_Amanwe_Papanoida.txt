'''Che Amanwe Papanoida''' was a [[Sexes|female]] [[Pantoran]] and the daughter of [[Baron]] [[Papanoida]] who served as [[Chairman]] of [[Pantora]].

==Biography==
[[File:CheAmanweBadass-SoI.png|left|thumb|200px|Che Amanwe uses an appropriated blaster to defend her family]]
During the [[Clone Wars]], Che Amanwe and her sister [[Chi Eekway Papanoida|Chi Eekway]] were [[Kidnapping of the Papanoida daughters|kidnapped]] by [[Rodian]] [[bounty hunter]] [[Greedo]] at their [[Coruscant]] home. He was aided by a [[Gotal]] assistant, who grabbed an unsuspecting Papanoida, followed by her sister. The two were separated, and with the help of [[Pantoran]] [[Senator]] [[Riyo Chuchi]] and [[Jedi]] [[Padawan]] [[Ahsoka Tano]], Chi Eekway Papanoida was rescued from Separatist clutches.
