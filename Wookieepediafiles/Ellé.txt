'''Ellé''' was a [[Human/Legends|Human]] [[Sexes/Legends|female]] from [[Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] who served as one of [[Senator/Legends|Senator]] [[Padmé Amidala/Legends|Padmé Amidala's]] [[handmaiden]]s at the end of the [[Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]].

==Biography==
In [[19 BBY/Legends|19 BBY]], Ellé served with her fellow [[handmaiden]], [[Moteé/Legends|Moteé]], with whom she had a romantic relationship. She was one of the select few who knew of [[Padmé Amidala/Legends|Amidala's]] secret [[Wedding of Anakin Skywalker and Padmé Amidala/Legends|marriage]] to the [[Jedi/Legends|Jedi]] [[Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Anakin Skywalker]].<ref name="DB" />

==Behind the scenes==
Ellé was portrayed by Australian actress [[Chantal Freer]] in a deleted scene of [[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']].<ref name="SWI71">{{InsiderCite|71|Prequel Update}}</ref>
