|apprentices=}}'''Eirtaé''' was a [[Sexes|female]] [[human]] who was part of [[Queen]] [[Padmé Amidala|Amidala's]] retinue of [[Naboo Royal Handmaidens|handmaidens]].

==Biography==

===Early Life===
Eirtaé was born on Naboo prior to the Invasion of Naboo. She joined the Naboo Royal Handmaidens by the time Queen Amidala was elected as the Queen of the Naboo.<ref name="TPM" />

===Naboo Crisis===
In [[32 BBY]],<ref name="Galactic Atlas">''[[Star Wars: Galactic Atlas]]''</ref> Eirtae was on [[Naboo]] when the [[planet]] was [[Invasion of Naboo|invaded]] by the [[Trade Federation]]. After [[Monarch of Naboo|queen]] [[Padmé Amidala]] was captured, [[Viceroy of the Trade Federation|Viceroy]] [[Nute Gunray]] told Amidala that [[Sexes|he]] was going to force her to sign a treaty to make the Federation's invasion legal. After Amidala refused to cooperate, Amidala, [[Governor]] [[Sio Bibble]], the [[Royal Naboo Security Forces|Naboo Security Forces]] and the [[Naboo Royal Handmaidens|handmaidens]] were to be taken by [[b1 battle droid]]s to [[Camp Four]]. As they made their way to the camp, [[Jedi Master]] [[Qui-Gon Jinn]] and his [[Padawan]], [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]] rushed in and saved the group and they fled to the [[transport]]s so that they could go to [[Coruscant]] Amidala could plea her case to the [[Galactic Senate|Senate]]. After a brief stop on Coruscant, Amidala decided to go to Naboo and free [[Naboo (people)|her people]] herself. She went to the [[Gungan]]s and begged for their help. The Gungan leader, [[Boss]] [[Rugor Nass|Nass]], agreed to help. Later, after planning the [[Battle of Naboo|liberation]], they made their way into the [[Theed Royal Palace|Theed palace]]. Inside, they ran into [[Maul|Darth Maul]] and they agreed to let Jinn deal with them while they fight the Federation [[droid]]s. After the battle, Eirtae attended Jinn's [[Funeral of Qui-Gon Jinn|funeral]], who had been [[Death|killed]] by Maul during the battle. Eirtae also attended the celebration of the liberation of Naboo shortly after.<ref name="TPM" />

==Personality and Traits==
Contrarily to all the other handmaidens and to the Queen herself, she had [[Color|blond]] [[hair]] and blue [[eye]]s. She also had fair skin.<ref name="TPM" />

==Behind the scenes==
Eirtaé was portrayed by [[Liz Wilson|Friday "Liz" Wilson]] in ''[[Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace|The Phantom Menace]]''.<ref name="TPM" />
