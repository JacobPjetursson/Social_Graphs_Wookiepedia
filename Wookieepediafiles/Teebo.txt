'''Teebo''' was an [[Ewok]] that was present during the [[Battle of Endor]].
One of [[Chief]] [[Chirpa]]'s best hunters alongside [[Asha Fahn]], they supported Chirpa's decision to help the Rebels take out the [[Death Star's shield generator|Imperial shield generator]], vetoing [[Logray]]'s choice to stay out of it. That night, he would write a message to be sent out to neighboring Ewok tribes to call them all to action against the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], that in the original [[Ewokese]] even Logray found beautiful.<ref name="ROTJ" />

Teebo was played in [[Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi|''Star Wars'' Episode VI ''Return of the Jedi'']] by [[Jack Purvis]]. Although not named in dialogue, Teebo was named in the movie's credits.<ref name="ROTJ" />
