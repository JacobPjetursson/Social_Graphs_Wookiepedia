'''Jobal Naberrie''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] from the [[planet]] [[Naboo]]. A member of the [[Naberrie family]],<ref name="Force Collection" /> she was the [[Parent|mother]] of [[Sola Naberrie]] and [[Padmé Amidala]].<ref name="Card Trader">[[Star Wars: Card Trader|''Star Wars'': Card Trader]]</ref> Her younger daughter, who was [[Reproduction|born]] Padmé Naberrie before assuming the name Amidala, served as [[Monarch of Naboo|queen]] and later as the [[Senator|representative]] of their [[homeworld]] in the [[Galactic Senate]] during [[Fall of the Republic|final years]] of the [[Galactic Republic]].<ref name="Padme Databank">{{Databank|padme-amidala|Padmé Amidala}}</ref>

==Biography==
===Early life===
At some point Jobal married a man named [[Ruwee Naberrie]] and they had two daughters, named [[Sola Naberrie|Sola]]<ref name="Visual Encyclopedia">''[[Star Wars: The Visual Encyclopedia]]''</ref> and [[Padmé Amidala|Padmé Naberrie]].<ref name="BioGal1" >{{Databank|padm-amidala-biography-gallery|Padmé Amidala Biography Gallery}} {{C|Image 1 caption}}</ref> When Padmé turned [[26 BBY|fourteen years]], she [[Coronation of Queen Amidala|was]] [[Democracy|elected]] [[Monarch of Naboo|Queen of Naboo]] and adopted the name of "Padmé Amidala".<ref name="BioGal1" />

===Death of Padmé===
[[19 BBY|Three years later]], following the end of the [[Clone Wars]] and the execution of [[Order 66]], Jobal, Ruwee and Sola attended the [[Funeral of Padmé Amidala|funeral]] of Padmé after her [[death]] at [[Polis Massa]].<ref name="Episode III" />

==Personality and traits==
Jobal Naberrie had brown [[hair]], brown [[eye]]s, and light skin.<ref name="Episode III" />

==Behind the scenes==
In deleted scenes for [[Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones|''Star Wars'': Episode II ''Attack of the Clones'']], Jobal Naberrie was established as the mother of Padmé Amidala.<ref name="Episode II deleted scenes">[[Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones|''Star Wars'': Episode II ''Attack of the Clones'']] deleted scenes</ref> She was portrayed by [[Trisha Noble]], who reprised the role for Amidala's funeral in [[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']].<ref name="IMDb">{{Imdb|url=name/nm0633682/|text=Trisha Noble}}</ref>
