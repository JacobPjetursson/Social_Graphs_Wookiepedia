'''Yaddle''', a [[Force-sensitive]] [[Sexes|female]] member of the [[Yoda's species|same]] [[species]] as [[Grand Master]] [[Yoda]], was a [[Jedi Master]] and member of the [[Jedi High Council]] during the last [[Standard year|years]] of the [[Galactic Republic]]. During the [[Invasion of Naboo]], Yaddle and her fellow councilors were introduced to the [[Chosen One]] [[Anakin Skywalker]]. After the [[Battle of Naboo]], she took a less active role in the [[Jedi Order]]'s affairs and was no longer on the Council by the [[time]] of the [[Clone Wars]].

==Biography==
Yaddle was [[Reproduction|born]] around [[509 BBY]], [[Standard year|years]] before the [[Battle of Yavin]].<ref name="Character Encyclopedia" /> At some point during [[Sexes|her]] life, she was found to be [[Force-sensitive]], and was taken to the [[Imperial Palace|Jedi Temple]] to be [[Jedi training|trained]] in the ways of the [[Jedi]]. Eventually, she was promoted to the rank of [[Jedi Master]], and was given a seat on the [[Jedi High Council]] on which she remained for some [[time]]. She also trained the [[Thisspiasian]] [[Oppo Rancisis]], who eventually took up a seat alongside her in the Jedi Council as well.<ref name="Character Encyclopedia" />

[[File:YaddleJediCouncil.jpeg|thumb|left|250px|Yaddle sits on the Jedi Council]]
During the [[Invasion of Naboo]], Yaddle was present in the [[Jedi Council Chamber]] inside the Jedi Temple on [[Coruscant]], when fellow Jedi Master [[Qui-Gon Jinn]] presented himself to the Council with daunting news that the Sith, the ancient enemies of the Jedi has returned to [[the galaxy]]. She was also present during the evaluation of [[Anakin Skywalker]], a 9-year-old Force-sensitive [[Youngling|child]], who Jinn believed was the [[Chosen One]] that was prophesied to bring balance to [[the Force]]. The Council initially agreed that they would not accept him into the [[Jedi Order]], however, they reversed their decision as Jinn's [[Death|dying]] wish was for Skywalker to be trained.<ref name="Episode I" />

Following the [[Battle of Naboo]], Yaddle decided to take a less active role in Jedi affairs<ref name="Ultimate Star Wars">''[[Ultimate Star Wars]]''</ref> and left the Jedi High Council by the time of the [[Clone Wars]].<ref name="Databank" /> Her seat on the Council was taken by Master Shaak Ti.{{Fact}}

==Personality and traits==
Yaddle was highly sought after for her great wisdom,<ref name="SW:AEYNTK">''[[Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know]]''</ref> and was also known for her quietness,<ref name="Databank" /> patience and kindness.<ref name="Journey Through Space">[[Journey Through Space (2015)|''Journey Through Space'' (2015)]]</ref> A [[Sexes|female]] member of the same [[Yoda's species|mysterious species]] as [[Yoda]],<ref name="Databank" /> Yaddle had [[Color|auburn]] [[hair]], green-gold [[eye]]s, green skin<ref name="Episode I" /> and a height of 0.6 [[meter]]s.<ref name="Databank" />

==Powers and abilities==
Yaddle was skilled in little-known [[Force power|Jedi powers]]<ref name="SW:AEYNTK" /> such as [[morichro]], an ancient art that enabled the user to slow the opponent's bodily functions to the point of death.<ref name="Character Encyclopedia" />

==Behind the scenes==
Yaddle was operated in the [[1999]] film [[Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace|''Star Wars'': Episode I ''The Phantom Menace'']] by [[Phil Eason]].<ref name="Card Trader">''[[Star Wars: Card Trader]]''</ref> The model for Yaddle was originally created by concept artist [[Iain McCaig]] as a sketch for a young Yoda, but was later put in the movies as Yaddle, a female member of Yoda's species.<ref name="Original Databank">{{DB|character|yaddle|Yaddle}}</ref>
