'''Sola Naberrie''' was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] from [[Naboo]]. A member of the [[Naberrie family]], she was a daughter of [[Ruwee Naberrie|Ruwee]] and [[Jobal Naberrie]], the sister of [[Padmé Amidala]], and the [[Parent|mother]] of [[Ryoo Naberrie|Ryoo]] and [[Pooja Naberrie]].

==Biography==
Sola Naberrie was the daughter of [[Ruwee Naberrie]] and his [[Marriage|wife]], [[Jobal Naberrie]]. Her sister was [[Padmé Amidala]], the [[senator]] of [[Naboo]] who married the [[Jedi Knight]] [[Anakin Skywalker]] in secret. By the [[Empire Day|end]] of the [[Clone Wars]], her sister [[Death|died]] moments after giving birth to the [[twin]]s [[Luke Skywalker]] and [[Leia Organa]]. Following her sister's death, Naberrie attended Amidala's [[Funeral of Padmé Amidala|funeral]] in [[Theed]] along with her [[Youngling|children]], [[Ryoo Naberrie|Ryoo]] and [[Pooja Naberrie|Pooja]], as well as other members of their [[Naberrie family|family]].<ref name="Episode III" />

==Personality and traits==
Sola Naberrie was a [[human]] [[Sexes|female]] who had light skin.<ref name="Episode III" />

==Behind the scenes==
Sola Naberrie was portrayed by [[Claudia Karvan]],<ref name="SW.com">{{SWArchive|url=episode-iii/bts/production/news20030829.html|text=Episode III Casting: Mother & Daughters}}</ref> an [[Wikipedia:Australians|Australian]] actress perhaps most well-known for her work on TV programs ''[[Wikipedia:The Secret Life of Us|The Secret Life of Us]]'' and ''[[Wikipedia:Love My Way|Love My Way]]''.<ref name="WP">{{WP|Claudia Karvan}}</ref> She first appeared in a scene from [[Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones|''Star Wars'': Episode II ''Attack of the Clones'']], but this scene was deleted and is not considered [[canon]]. She appears at the end of [[Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith|''Star Wars'': Episode III ''Revenge of the Sith'']] attending Padmé Amidala's funeral. The character's name is given in the film's closing credits.
